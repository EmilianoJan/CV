Últimos trabajos

Photothermal microscope (Research)

Thermal diffusivity measurement equipment using a technique developed in the laboratory. The only equipment in the world that performs micrometric measurements of thermal diffusivity on any surface in a non-destructive manner. The equipment can measure thermal diffusivity, hardness and concentration of compounds. The properties are of interest since they can predict oxidation of welds, inhomogeneities in nuclear pellets, among others.
